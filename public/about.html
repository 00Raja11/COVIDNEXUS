About Covid-19

What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2). It primarily spreads through droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or exhales.

How it Spreads

  • Through respiratory droplets from coughs, sneezes, or talking
  • By touching contaminated surfaces and then touching eyes, nose, or mouth
  • Close contact with infected people

Why Awareness is Important

Being aware of the facts helps in taking preventive measures, reducing panic, and stopping the spread of misinformation. Staying informed ensures better protection for yourself and your community.
